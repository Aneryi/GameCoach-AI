# Alpha Complete Guide

## Character Overview

Alpha is a forgiving damage-dealer with consistent DPS, self-cleanse, and stable laning. Ideal for players looking to climb steadily.

## Core Mechanics

### Passive — Sustained Output
Every third attack triggers bonus damage. Maintain attack range for continuous pressure.

### Skill 1 — Primary Clear
Main wave-clear and poke tool. Use for harass in lane, but never miss last-hits for poke damage.

### Skill 2 — Self-Cleanse (Core)
**Alpha's most important skill**: removes crowd control and repositions. Always save for survival, never use to chase. In teamfights, use after the enemy commits their CC.

### Ultimate — Long-Range Execute
Ranged finisher and CC. Hitting enemy carries deals massive damage. Aim for the enemy damage dealer in teamfights.

## Lane Phase

### Early (1-8 min)
- Farm consistently; don't miss CS for harass
- Enemy jungler missing → retreat to tower immediately
- Always save Skill 2, never use for wave clear

### Mid (8-15 min)
- Group with team, don't solo side-lane
- Watch river bushes after 10 minutes
- Maintain safe distance when pushing towers

### Late (15+ min)
- One positioning mistake can lose the game
- Take zero unnecessary risks
- Let teammates initiate, you follow up from safety

## Matchups

### Alpha counters
- Short-range mages (range advantage)
- Immobile characters

### Counters Alpha
- Stealth assassins (invisible engage)
- High-burst divers
- Mobile skirmishers

## Daily Training

1. Practice mode: Skill 2 cleanse timing (10 min)
2. First 3 ranked games: Alpha only, record death causes
3. Post-game review: How many times did you use Skill 2 correctly in teamfights?

# Bravo Advanced Guide

## Character Overview

Bravo is a burst damage-dealer who relies on mobility skill resets and enhanced attacks for high damage output. Suitable for players with some experience; needs steady early farming.

## Core Mechanic

Bravo's damage comes from the enhanced attack after using Skill 1. The enhanced attack deals bonus damage and reduces Skill 1 cooldown. Core cycle: Dash → Enhanced Attack → Auto → Auto → Dash.

## Skill 1 Usage

### Dash Direction Choices
- **Lateral dash**: dodge skills + maintain output — most common
- **Diagonal forward**: chase low-HP targets
- **Backward dash**: escape when dived
- **Don't auto immediately after dash**: sometimes reposition first, then attack

### Common Mistakes
- Dashing into the enemy team → guaranteed death
- Dashing into unwarded bushes → potential ambush
- Auto-attacking immediately after every dash → reveals position

## Recommended Build

### Standard
Basic_Boots → Core_Item → Crit_Core → Burst_Item → Pen_Item → Revive_Item

### Behind
Swap boots for Tenacity_Boots, replace Burst_Item with Armor_Item for durability.

## Early Game is Critical

Bravo is a classic "mid-game spike" character. Early game has one job: **survive to 8 minutes safely**.

### Early Priorities
1. Don't miss CS (most important)
2. Don't die (more important than getting a kill)
3. Don't force trades (unless jungler ganks)
4. Don't push without minimap vision

## Game Phases

### Early (1-8 min)
Focus on farming. Only trade when jungler ganks. Enemy jungler missing → retreat.

### Mid (8-15 min)
Power spike phase. Group for objectives. Use dash for safe DPS angles. Hit once, reposition, repeat.

### Late (15+ min)
Massive damage but extremely fragile. Positioning matters more than ever. Never initiate unless completely safe.

## Matchups

### Bravo counters
- Immobile backliners
- Tanks (high percent-health damage)

### Counters Bravo
- Stealth assassins (invisible engage into one-shot)
- Point-click lockdown ultimates
- High-mobility divers

## Training Focus

1. **Dash direction practice**: Practice mode — all 4 directions with timing
2. **Economy focus**: Target top-2 team economy by 8 minutes
3. **Death review**: Record and analyze first 3 deaths every game

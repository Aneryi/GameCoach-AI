# General Climbing Methodology

## Mindset

### Three Core Climbing Truths
1. **Dying less > Killing more** — Every death feeds enemy resources and tempo
2. **Don't blame teammates** — Every game has lessons; blame keeps you stuck
3. **Volume creates quality** — 5 focused games/day > 20 weekend spam games

### Loss Streak Protocol
- Lose 2 in a row → 5-minute break, review last game
- Lose 3 in a row → Stop for the day
- Mentally tilted → Stop regardless of result

## Technical Skills

### Fundamentals (Daily Practice)
1. **Last-hitting**: 10 minutes practice mode, zero missed CS
2. **Minimap**: Check every 5 seconds until it's automatic
3. **Pings**: Build muscle memory for communication pings

### Advanced
1. **Cooldown tracking**: Know approximate CDs of key enemy abilities
2. **Position prediction**: Infer enemy jungler location from minimap
3. **Economy management**: Know when to farm and when to group

## Review Process

### Post-Game Three Questions
1. Could I have avoided my first 3 deaths?
2. Did I ever fight without teammates present?
3. Was my economy dragging the team down?

### Review Tools
- Watch replays from your own perspective only
- Mark what you were doing 10 seconds before each death
- Count: how many times did you cross river without minimap vision?

## Growth Path

### Week 1: Reduce Mistakes
- Goal: Average deaths < 5 per game
- Training: CS practice + minimap awareness
- Forbidden: Blind side-laning, pushing without vision

### Week 2: Build Consistency
- Goal: Win rate back to 50%
- Training: Lock 2 characters, review every game
- Forbidden: Constantly switching characters, trying unfamiliar roles

### Week 3-4: Break Through
- Goal: 5 consecutive stable performances
- Training: Learn 1 new role, watch pro player VODs
- Forbidden: Overconfidence on win streak, mental boom on loss streak

## Daily Training Templates

### 30-Minute Session
- 5 min: Practice mode CS
- 20 min: One ranked game (focus on one training goal)
- 5 min: Review (write 3 improvements)

### 60-Minute Session
- 10 min: Practice mode CS + skill drills
- 40 min: Two ranked games
- 10 min: Review + next-day plan

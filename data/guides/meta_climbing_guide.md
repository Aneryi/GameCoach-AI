# Meta Analysis and Climbing Guide

## Current Meta Overview

The current meta (2026.06) emphasizes **self-sufficiency, mid-game presence, and consistent execution** for damage roles.

## Meta Tier List

### Top Tier (Meta-Defining)
1. **Alpha** — Self-cleanse + consistent DPS, highest forgiveness
2. **Bravo** — Massive burst once items come online, mid-game spike
3. **Charlie** — Exceptional kiting, strong in skirmish-heavy games

### Strong Tier (Solid Choices)
- Delta: High sustained damage but lacks mobility, needs team protection
- Echo: Late-game monster but weak early, requires safe farming

### Weak in Current Meta
- Characters without mobility tools
- Characters with slow wave clear and long ramp-up times

## Item Changes

### Buffed
- **Range_Bow**: Attack range bonus increased, must-buy for damage dealers
- **Core_Item**: Percent-health damage buffed, better vs tanks

### Nerfed
- **Attack_Speed_Item**: Proc rate reduced, no longer optimal

## Climbing Strategy

### Low → Mid Rank
1. Lock in 2-3 meta-strong characters
2. Reduce blind map movements
3. Focus on CS and safe farming

### Mid → High Rank
1. Learn to adapt builds to enemy composition
2. Identify enemy assassin position before every fight
3. Review positioning after every teamfight

### High → Top Rank
1. Master characters in at least 2 roles
2. Process minimap information efficiently
3. Team communication: calls, cooldown tracking, engage timing

## Core Meta Advice

1. **Pick characters first, items second**: don't autopilot the same build every game
2. **When behind, build defense**: survival enables comeback
3. **Builds are dynamic**: 3 AP threats → must buy magic resist; full AD team → armor item
4. **Don't force weak characters**: even mastery can't overcome a nerfed character
